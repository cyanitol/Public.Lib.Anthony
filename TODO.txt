  roadmap backlog, and TODO feature gaps in `doc/TODO.txt` are materially reduced

   smallest hot-path functions that are already well covered
2. Add package-local test helpers to collapse repeated MC/DC and coverage scaffolding
3. Re-run `gocyclo -over 8 .` after each slice and track the remaining overages
